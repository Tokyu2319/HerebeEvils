A state where a [soul](./01%20Beings.md) is without [mind](./01%20Beings.md) and [vessel](./01%20Beings.md). In this state the soul cannot gain anymore experience and if forcefully put into a new vessel will create a new [identity](../04%20The%20Paradigms%20of%20Reality/4.3%20Identity.md) with no memories belonging to the previous [being](./01%20Beings.md). Souls normally enter this state when the vessel is destroyed and the mind subsequently collapses. This phenomenon is known as **death**.  
  
Transient souls do not obey the laws governing matter and energy, interacting with the universe only through [mana](../03%20General%20Thaumics/03.01%20Magic/01%20Mana.md).  
  
Transient souls become significant when the universe reaches its [end](../01%20The%20Laws%20of%20Reality/03%20The%20End.md) or when it’s being used for magical operations.

### Addendum  
At the time of writing, approximately 106 humans die for each minute on Earth[^1][^2]. Note that we’re only counting humans as they have more [soul value](./02%20Experience.md) and with that they can reflect more [phana](../03%20General%20Thaumics/03.01%20Magic/01%20Mana.md). The passing of humans can be visualized by illuminating Earth with phana. Since transient souls do not obey gravity, it is seen that the Earth is leaving a trail of souls. This goes for every world with a population of sentient beings.  
  
[^1]: [Based on Earth-derived estimates](https://www.worldometers.info/)  
[^2]: [Earth (third planet from its star)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth)
