There’s two distinct types of memories, the [vessel](./01%20Beings.md)’s memory and the [mind](./01%20Beings.md)’s memory.  
  
The vessel’s memory is the capacity to store and recall information. connecting synapses and data in a hardware are information inside a vessel’s cognition.  
  
The mind’s memory is what I define as a being’s [identity](../04%20The%20Paradigms%20of%20Reality/4.3%20Identity.md). **During the process of interpreting [experiences](./02%20Experience.md) the mind will form memories in itself as a byproduct**. These memories are separate from the vessel’s memories as they can be manipulated by [manon](../03%20General%20Thaumics/03.01%20Magic/01%20Mana.md).  
  
The memories of both aspects are lost when those aspects are destroyed, naturally, the mind will die out when there’s no compatible vessel. **Recovering a soul’s mind and its memories is possible due to the existence of [resurrection magic](../03%20General%20Thaumics/03.04%20Thaumic%20Collection/Resurrection%20Magic.md).**
