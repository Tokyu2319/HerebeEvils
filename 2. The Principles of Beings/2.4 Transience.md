A state where a [soul](./2.1%20Beings.md) is without [mind](./2.1%20Beings.md) and [vessel](./2.1%20Beings.md). In this state the soul cannot gain anymore experience and if forcefully put into a new vessel will create a new [identity](../4.%20The%20Paradigms%20of%20Reality/4.3%20Identity.md) with no memories belonging to the previous [being](./2.1%20Beings.md). Souls normally enter this state when the vessel is destroyed and the mind subsequently collapses. This phenomenon is known as **death**.  
  
Transient souls do not obey the laws governing matter and energy, interacting with the universe only through [mana](../3.%20Magic/3.1%20Mana.md).  
  
Transient souls become significant when the universe reaches its [end](../1.%20The%20Laws%20of%20Reality/1.3%20The%20End.md) or when it’s being used for magical operations.

### Addendum  
At the time of writing, approximately 106 humans die for each minute on Earth[^1][^2]. Note that we’re only counting humans as they have more [soul value](./2.2%20Experience.md) and with that they can reflect more [phana](../3.%20Magic/3.1%20Mana.md). This phenomenon can be visualized by illuminating Earth with phana.. Since transient souls do not obey gravity, it is seen that the Earth is leaving a trail of souls. This goes for every world with a population of sentient beings.
