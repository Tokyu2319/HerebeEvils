> This topic will explain how the [mana triad](./Mana.md) and created matter interact with each other.  
# Interactions Between The Mana Triad  
- ## Raw Mana Interaction  
	- Mana + Phana = More Phana, more luminosity or/and more heat.  
	- Mana + Manon = intense force.  
	- Mana + Armana = Mana decays slower while armana becomes more tangible/solidified.  
- ## Phana Interaction  
	- Phana + Mana = More Phana, more luminosity or/and more heat.  
	- Phana + Manon = More focus and control on beam and wavelengths.  
	- Phana + Armana = Inert but intense heat can disrupt the armana structure.  
- ## Manon Interaction  
	- Manon + Mana = intense force.  
	- Manon + Phana = Allows focus and control over beam and wavelengths.  
	- Manon + Armana = Allows changes in Armana structure and complexity.  
- ## Armana Interaction  
	- Armana + Mana = Mana decays slower while armana becomes more tangible/solidified.  
	- Armana + Phana = Inert but intense heat can disrupt the armana structure.  
	- Armana + Manon = Allows changes in Armana structure and complexity.  
# Matter Interaction  
- ## Raw Mana Interaction  
	It is inert, it phases through materials.  
- ## Phana interaction  
	Inert but intense heat may cause matter to change phases such as melting.  
- ## Manon Interaction

# Spell Examples  
- ## Flamethrower  
	A mage creates flammable gasses from their palms in which they control their movement using manons, they then ignite the gasses using a burst of hot phana. They continually create gasses to fuel the flame until their enemy is burned.  
- ## Lightning  
	The mage stretches manon over the field of influence, this can easily be disrupted by opposing mages.
