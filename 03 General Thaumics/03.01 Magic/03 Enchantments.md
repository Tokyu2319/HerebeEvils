A technique to configure bonded [armana](./01%20Mana.md) to automate or complete magical tasks. Enchantments can be classified by their level of complexity.  
# Simple Enchantments  
Armana structures that lack design and programs.  
These structures are created on the go, mostly to be used as:  
- a shield,  
- a weapon,  
- a platform,  
- and more.  
# Configured Enchantments  
Armana structures that assist in completing magical tasks. These structures are prepared in advance to assist in spellcasting, reducing the caster’s mental load.  
These armana structures are typically implemented on:  
- Staves,  
- Tomes,  
- Books,  
- and more  
# Programmed Enchantments  
Armana structures that follow a predefined program when supplied with mana. These structures can cast spells autonomously according to that program, allowing the caster to simply provide mana for them to function.  
These armana structures are found in:  
- magical defense systems,  
- [teleporters](../../Teleportation.md),  
- [portals](../03.04%20Thaumic%20Collection/Portals.md),  
- [world framework](../03.04%20Thaumic%20Collection/World%20Framework.md)s, <!--I can’t be bothered to add more aliases for plurals-->   
- and more.  
# Autonomous Enchantments  
Armana structures **approaching** the [complexities](../../02%20The%20Principles%20of%20Beings/01%20Beings.md) required of a mind. These structures are able to run indefinitely provided they have a sufficient mana supply. However, when this complexity becomes **identical** to that of a mind, autonomous enchantments may emerge as their own—artificial—beings, though those beings may still be limited by their programming.

# Examples of Enchantments  
- ## Mana Batteries  
	Mana batteries are enchantments where the armana structure is designed to prolong or pause the decay of mana making it so that mana can be used at the ready or to power other enchantments.  
- ## Staves, Tomes, Books, and More  
	These are enchantments where the armana structure is designed so that spell casting is easier or requires less focus. This makes it easier for mages who are casting multiple spells requiring detailed attention.  
- ## Magical Shields and Smart Shields  
	Magical shields are enchantments where the armana structure is designed so it releases more armana for the purpose of blocking or disrupting physical or magical attacks. The object may be the shield itself reinforced with armana serving as a protective layer, or the object as a trinket that releases a layer of armana.  
	  
	Magical shields offer an impenetrable air-tight defense but that’s actually bad if you need to see or breathe. Smart shields are designed in a way that they can distinguish what’s harmful or not, this includes, heat, toxic gas, physical attacks, and more.

- ## Magic Disruptors  
	Magic disruptors are enchantments where the armana structure is designed so it releases [manon](./01%20Mana.md) and/or [phana](./01%20Mana.md) to disrupt spell casting. Making it so that conventional means to harm are effective against crippled mages.  
- ## Vacuum-Displacement Chambers & Pressure Bladders  
	On the topic of creation magic, vacuum-displacement chambers (VDCs) and pressure bladders (PBs) are containers that are enchanted to allow easy use of creation magic.  
  
	VDCs are industrial/large scale vacuum chambers filled with perfluoropolyethers (PFPEs) as the medium for creation. Creating matter needs a vessel for the matter to be created from so [mana share](./01%20Mana.md) is used on the VDC and the PFPE as extensions of a vessel in order to allow creation magic. PFPEs are fluorinated oils where every hydrogen is replaced with fluorine and they’re used in spacecraft mechanisms. PFPEs have the following properties of near-zero vapor pressure, chemically inert, vacuum clean, and stays liquid even at high and low temperatures. PFPEs are perfect for creation as they can be pushed when matter is created in the vacuum chamber which will then occupy the empty space above.  
	VDCs are used for sourcing large amounts of precious materials or the creation of large machinery.  
  
	PBs are small-scale sealed elastic membranes filled completely with liquids. PBs can be used to create what VDCs already create but they are mostly used to create biological materials. PBs are disposable as PBs are made to be teared opened once creation is done. water balloons are considered technically pressure bladders.
