Type: Light Source  
  
Description:  
The projection of [phana](../../03.01%20Magic/01%20Mana.md) to produce light.  
  
Mechanism:  
Phana particles are projected from the user’s [vessels](../../../02%20The%20Principles%20of%20Beings/01%20Beings.md), emitting light that illuminates surrounding areas.  
  
Knowledge Requirements:  
- Basic understanding of [mana](../../03.01%20Magic/01%20Mana.md) conversion to phana  
  
Use Cases:  
- Identifying the presence of a [transient](../../../02%20The%20Principles%20of%20Beings/04%20Transience.md) soul  
  
Limitations:  
Requires [manon](../../03.01%20Magic/01%20Mana.md) for spectrum manipulation  
  
Notability:  
Though simple in application, this spell demonstrates the separation between phana emission and manon-based control.
