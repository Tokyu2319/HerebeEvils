Type: Matter Materialization  
  
Description:  
The conversion of [mana](../../03.01%20Magic/01%20Mana.md) into fluid matter, created from the user’s [vessel](../../../02%20The%20Principles%20of%20Beings/01%20Beings.md) either internally or externally  
  
Mechanism:  
Mana is converted into matter and distributed in a non-rigid state, forming fluids without a fixed shape from the user’s vessel. The lack of structural constraints reduces the complexity required for stable materialization.  
  
Knowledge Requirements:  
1. Basic understanding of mana conversion into matter  
  
Use Cases:  
- Creation of water, oxygen, or similar fluids  
- Nutrient satiation  
  
Limitations:  
Composition is limited without additional information  
  
Notability:  
Serves as a foundational example of mana-to-matter conversion, demonstrating that stable materialization is significantly easier in non-rigid forms.
