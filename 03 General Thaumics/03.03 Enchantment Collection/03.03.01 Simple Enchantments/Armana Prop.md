Type: Structural Formation  
  
Description:  
[Armana](../../03.01%20Magic/01%20Mana.md) particles bonded together to form simple shapes.  
  
Mechanism:  
The user bonds armana to take on a variety of shapes. Whether the user can bond each individual particle depends on their skill.  
  
Knowledge Requirements:  
- Basic understanding of [mana](../../03.01%20Magic/01%20Mana.md) conversion to armana  
- Basic understanding of armana bonding  
  
Use Cases:  
- Summoning of platforms for traversal  
- Summoning of tools  
- Summoning of melee weapons  
- Summoning of shields  
  
Limitations:  
Further understanding is needed for armana configuration  
  
Notability:  
Serves as a foundational example of armana bonding
