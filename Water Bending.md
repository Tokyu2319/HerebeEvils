Type: Manipulation  
  
Description:  
The application of [manon](./03%20General%20Thaumics/03.01%20Magic/01%20Mana.md) to exert force on cohesive flowing matter.  
  
Mechanism:  
Manon particles are projected from the user’s [vessel](./02%20The%20Principles%20of%20Beings/01%20Beings.md) to apply mechanical influence on liquids such as water.  
  
Knowledge requirements:  
- Basic understanding of [mana](./03%20General%20Thaumics/03.01%20Magic/01%20Mana.md) conversion to manon  
- Basic understanding of manon agency  
  
Use Cases:  
- Lifting and moving liquids  
  
Limitations:  
Force output scales with manon density  
Susceptible to disruption by external manon under another user’s control, particularly when the area of influence is large or sustained over distance.  
  
Notability:  
A conceptual limitation where the user interprets force application as “elemental control,” restricting the true versatility of manon. Harder in execution compared to Earth Bending because of liquid’s non-rigidity.
