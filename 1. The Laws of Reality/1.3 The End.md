The end of the universe happens when the universe is unable to sustain anymore life and the last living [being](../2.%20The%20Principles%20of%20Beings/2.1%20Beings.md) dies. Changes only happen during the start of [the heat death of the universe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heat_death_of_the_universe), after that every soul of every being that has ever lived will eventually [resurrect](../3.%20Magic/3.9%20Resurrection%20Magic.md) each other and destroy each other. Instead of normal resurrection where only the [identity](../2.%20The%20Principles%20of%20Beings/2.3%20Memory.md) of a soul is restored, during this event the resurrected souls will gain more than their original soul value and this ramps up.  
  
When the eventual time comes, the last soul to survive this event will become what I define as a god. Having lived the lives of every being, this god is eternally content with their godhood and they’ll eventually make the choice of recreating the universe using [creation magic](../3.%20Magic/3.3%20Creation%20Magic.md)[.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Big_Bang) They’ll also have the choice to use [resurrection](../3.%20Magic/3.9%20Resurrection%20Magic.md) on themselves into destruction or regression.
