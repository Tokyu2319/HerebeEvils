User: The Temporal Traveler  
  
Description:  
The full control of time inside a [region](../../01%20The%20Laws%20of%20Reality/01%20The%20Universe.md). The user is able to control time either Region-wide or in a localized space. The following are the effects the user can give inside a space:  
- Stop its time  
- Rewind its time instantly or gradually  
- Accelerate its time instantly or gradually  
  
However, a resource is needed to allow this ability to work, Harbored Pain.  
  
Mechanism:  
- Harbored Pain  
	Harbored Pain is received and stored inside the user whenever the user receives interpreted pain, that is mental or physical pain. Harbored Pain is expended when the user uses their ability, expenditure scales with how much space was affected and how much time had passed (time passed refers to the rewinding and acceleration of time).  
  
- Time Stop  
	Time stop’s expenditure of Harbored Pain is negligible, a slap of the wrist already fulfills the requirements to stop time Region-wide. Light entering a time-stopped region halts at the boundary, accumulating as static energy, rendering the region visually void. Touching a time-stopped space from the outside would feel like a perfectly rigid surface.  
  
- Temporal Bubble  
	A tight bubble of active time surrounds the user, preserving a localized frame of causality. This allows movement within time-stopped space, at the cost of extreme spatial limitation and minimal air exchange. The user would be almost blind inside time-stopped space and flashes of visuals would appear as they let light enter the bubble.  
  
- Rewind  
	Rewind’s expenditure of Harbored Pain is significant, repeated sessions of torture fulfills the requirements to rewind an evaporated glass of water back to full. Rewind restores both the temporal state and spatial coordinates of a target, reverting it to its prior position within the region’s spacetime history.  
	  
	For example, rewinding a planet’s history would also rewind their placement in the universe, leaving the planet without a star if the user was only targeting the planet.  
	  
	The user can outright ignore the spatial coordinates of a target to restore the temporal state of a it, but this would come at massive cost of Harbored Pain.  
	  
	Time travel is achievable with rewind by targeting the region the user resides in.  
  
- Accelerate  
	Accelerate’s expenditure of Harbored Pain is significant, repeated sessions of torture fulfills the requirements to accelerate a full glass of water to be empty. Localized acceleration is intuitive, anything that happens inside an accelerated space happens because it does, accelerating an apple would also accelerate the processes of microorganisms inside the apple to decompose it.   
	  
	Time travel is achievable with acceleration by targeting the region the user resides in.  
  
Limitations:  
- Harbored Pain as a resource  
- Time stop is limited by lung capacity  
- Rewind is constrained due to its dependency on spatial-coordinates  
- Acceleration is inherently less versatile than rewind, as it cannot reconstruct prior states and is limited to advancing existing processes, often yielding unpredictable or undesirable outcomes.
