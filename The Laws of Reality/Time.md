# Eternalism  
All moments—past, present, and future—exist simultaneously. The past cannot change, the present moves ever forward, and the future exists as a traversable state.  
  
# Temporal Enforcement  
The universe will prevent any paradoxes from ever happening in time. An example would be: You’re walking around in the past with no intent to change the future—you can’t anyway but that’s besides the point—for some reason you trip and fall. It may look like an accident but that’s actually the universe preventing you from making a paradox
